DR. MADHU RAMESH, PH.D.

Postdoctoral Researcher

Dr. Madhu Ramesh completed his PhD in Chemical Biology at JNCASR, Bengaluru, India. Trained as a veterinarian with a master’s in Biochemistry, his research spans amyloid proteins, RNA biology, mitochondria, microglia, and neuroinflammation, focusing on Alzheimer’s disease. At the Craddock Lab, University of Waterloo, he explores quantum biology in Parkinson’s disease.
